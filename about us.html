About us:

The Star collective is an online archive gathering and documenting all found artwork of the late StarChild11, a well known artist from the DN2 community that vanished one day without a trace.


Who was Starchild11?

Starchild11 was a beloved member of the early original character artist community, often headlining many of the movements and controversies of the time. They mostly spent time in a small closed community called DelcNstwo, also known under the name DN2. They had quickly risen to be somewhat of a leader position, gathering a whole group of followers, who hung on their every word.

After their untimely death in 2012, most of their social media was deleted, leaving a massive void in internet history, especially the DN2 community. They had lost years of not only artwork, but also chat rooms, lore and historical posts.


Who are we at the Star Collective?

The Star collective was started by two sisters, who had spent a big part of their childhood on DN2, posting their artwork and making friends. They have many fond memories of the time. But recently, when they were trying to recall their time there and their friendship with Starchild11, they had found that most of those memories have faded away. For some reason, any recollection of this beloved community member was hazy and wrong. Many times the memories one of the sisters had would be contradictory to a memory the other had, leading to much confusion.

As such the sisters decided to start the Star collective, to track down any remaining content made by Starchild11, as a way to rebuild and figure out their childhood memories.

It is a long and arduous process, but one we at Star collective take with pride. DN2’s story deserves to be told. Starchild11 deserves to be known.
